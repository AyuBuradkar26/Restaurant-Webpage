✨About us 😊

RASRAJ Restaurant was formed in 1992 by Yash P.K. Since then we kept moving forward with one goal in mind.

At Rasraj, we believe in everything at its prime. Our focus is to bring the best and most responsibly sourced ingredients to the table. Our staunch belief in providing exceptional quality products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting has helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best hidden gems in Chandrapur,city.

🏆 Rasraj was recognized with three star in the 2019, 2020 & 2021 cleanliness guides.
